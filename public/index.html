Pierre Bardier

I am a Ph.D student in theoretical economics at Paris School of Economics and Ecole Normale Supérieure de Paris. My research spans economic design, with a specific focus on fair division, social choice and welfare economics, voting, and individual decision making. I am extremely happy to work under the supervision of Marc Fleurbaey.

Before my Ph.D, I was studying at Ecole Normale Supérieure de Paris for 5 years, during which I completed in particular a MSc in economics at Paris School of Economics and a MSc in mathematics at Paris-I-Panthéon-Sorbonne.
